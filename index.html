Welcome to Single Line Scrabble!

How to Play

Test your vocabulary skills and build high-scoring words in this single-player version of Scrabble.

Instructions

Drag and drop tiles from your rack onto the board to form words. The score for each word will be displayed.

You can refresh your tiles or reset the game at any time. Remember, this is a single-line version, so focus on building the longest word possible to maximize your score!

Good luck and have fun!
